Advanced Forensic Investigation

The Velocity Attack: 184 fraud transactions occurred within 60 seconds of a previous fraud. This highly indicates automated script (bot) attacks rather than manual card theft.
The Invisible Footprint: By isolating encrypted V-features, we found that V14 and V17 have completely different distributions for fraud. Fraudsters leave a distinct mathematical fingerprint.
The Isolation Forest: Reducing 28 dimensions into a 2D PCA map reveals that fraud transactions (Red) live in isolated clusters away from the mainland of normal transactions (Blue).
